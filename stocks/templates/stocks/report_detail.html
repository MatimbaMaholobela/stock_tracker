Analysis Methodology

This report analyses trading signals based on the 3% drop rule algorithm:

  • BUY Signal: Generated when a stock price drops 3% or more from the previous day
  • SELL Signal: Automatically triggered 5 days after a BUY signal
  • Success Rate: Percentage of BUY signals that were profitable 5 days later
  • Successful BUY: A BUY signal where the price increased within 5 days
